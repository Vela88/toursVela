Zapoteca Azuzena is an amazing restaurant located 40 minutes outside the city

Have you tried the Oaxacan Chocolate?

If you already did not know, Oaxaca is known for its Chocolate. This place has some really good Chocolate served in a traditional clay mug.

Note: You can take a taxi cab which charges apx. $$ or take a bus or "taxi colectivo" (which is like an uber pool but with a fixed route). It takes about 45 minutes to get to the restaurant from the city.

This restaurant is one of my favorites in Oaxaca. They offer great service and freshly made food cooked in a traditional kitchen. All the food and most drinks are served in traditional pottery made by angel y jacobo.

Highlights

  • Chocolate
  • Coffee
  • Memelas
  • Service
  • Ambiance

They have indoor and outdoor seating. theres also a garden and large soccer field where kids can run around. Check out the Alebrije Gallery which is also made by angel and jocobo. check out thier link
